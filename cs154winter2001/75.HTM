Using standard laws of first order logic, the formula

fq0(,w) & T => x y: fq-acc(x,y),

which is the key to the reduction, can be rewritten in the required form. In particular P => Q can be written as "not P or Q", "not forall x: P(x)" is equivalent to "exists x: not P(x)", and the existential quantifiers distribute outwards while renaming bound variables to be distinct.
